Known / accepted behavior
-------------------------
- At exactly 45° diagonals, quantizeWorldXZToCardinal tie-breaks toward depth (Z) first
  (see movementFromCamera.ts). Slight yaw drift near diagonals can switch a single intent
  between two cardinals; use snap90 mode in code if product wants stricter stability during
  free orbit (currently MazeGame uses "continuous").
